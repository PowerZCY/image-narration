We offer a **3-day money-back guarantee** for all first-time purchases of our paid plans.

### 2. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will **NOT** be issued in the following cases:
- More than 20% of purchased credits have been used
- Free trial credits, promotional credits, or bonus credits
- Purchase made more than 3 days ago
- Accounts suspended for violating our Terms of Service
- Users who have previously received a refund
